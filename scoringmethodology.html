3 — Alpha Autotrader Decisions (autotrader.js)

The autotrader runs at 9:35 AM ET. It evaluates every stock through three tiers in order. A stock must pass ALL three tiers before an order is placed.

Buy Decision — 3-Tier Funnel

Tier 3 — Market Regime (Hard Block — runs first)
Is the broad market safe to buy into?
Reads SPY from stock_signals table.
  • BULL: SPY above_200ma=true AND above_50ma=true → proceed to Tier 2
  • CAUTION: SPY above_200ma=true but above_50ma=false → proceed to Tier 2 with caution note
  • BEAR: SPY above_200ma=false → block ALL new entries
  • UNKNOWN: SPY not in stock_signals → block ALL new entries (add SPY to watchlist to fix)
Tier 2 — Quality Filter (Hard Block per symbol)
Does this stock meet minimum quality standards?
ALL four conditions must pass:
  • Score ≥ 50 (HOLD or better)
  • Price ≥ $5 (no penny stocks)
  • RSI ≤ 65 (not overbought — wider than the old ≤55 gate)
  • Price ≤ 50 SMA × 1.08 (not >8% above 50 SMA — mean reversion guard; extended stocks have poor risk/reward and tend to snap back)
Tier 1 — Entry Gate (Confirmations needed)
Is the timing right to buy now?
All three sub-checks must pass:
  • Score ≥ 65
  • ≥ 2 of 4 confirmations: RSI 30–65 · MACD bullish/above signal · Price above 50 SMA · Volume ≥ 1.3×
  • No earnings within 5 calendar days (Finnhub earnings calendar)
If all pass → order placed.

Sell Decision — evaluateExit()

Runs on all held Alpha positions at 9:35 AM and optionally at midday. Hard stop always takes priority.

Hard Stop — Immediate 100% Sell
Price ≤ entry price − 8%
Maximum loss per position is capped at 8%. No overrides. Fires immediately.
Soft Exits — 50% Sell (take partial profit / reduce risk)
Any one of these triggers a 50% position reduction:
  • Score < 25 (momentum lost — multiple signals turned negative)
  • RSI > 75 (overbought — take partial profit at extension)
  • EMA 9 crossed below EMA 21 ≤3 sessions ago (short-term momentum flip)
  • MACD bearish cross (trend turning negative)
  • Time stop: held ≥30 days AND gain < 5% (dead money — redeploy capital)

VIX Position Sizing

VIX is estimated from VIXY (VIXY × 1.8 + 2 ≈ VIX). Position size scales down as volatility rises:

VIX LevelSize MultiplierRationale
VIX < 20100%Calm market — full position.
VIX 20–2975%Elevated volatility — reduce size.
VIX ≥ 3050%Fear regime — half position to limit gap-down exposure.

Position Size Formula

perSlot = min(deployable / openSlots, maxPerPos)
shares = floor(perSlot / price)
